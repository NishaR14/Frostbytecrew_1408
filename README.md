MoodLight is a comprehensive mental wellness platform that combines AI-powered support, mood tracking, therapeutic tools, and community resources to help users navigate their emotional journey with compassion and care.

**Features**
AI Chat Support
Real-time Emotional Support: Powered by Groq's embedded API for instant, compassionate guidance
Confidential Conversations: Safe space to openly discuss concerns without judgment
Personalized Responses: Context-aware AI that adapts to your emotional state

**Daily Mood Tracking**
Emotional Pattern Recognition: Track daily moods to identify trends and triggers
Self-Awareness Insights: Visualize emotional patterns over time
Personalized Analytics: Understand what influences your emotional wellbeing

**Journaling & Reflection**
**Daily Journal Prompts:** Guided writing exercises for self-reflection

**Emotional Processing:** Structured templates for exploring feelings

**Progress Tracking:** Review past entries to observe personal growth

**Mindfulness Tools**
**Guided Breathing Exercises:** Calming techniques for immediate stress relief

**Meditation Sessions:** Curated meditation practices for anxiety reduction

**Mindfulness Reminders:** Daily prompts to stay present and grounded

**Community Support**
**Anxiety Support Circles:** Scheduled group sessions for shared experiences

**Safe Community Spaces:** Moderated groups for various mental health topics

**Peer Support:** Connect with others on similar wellness journeys

**Crisis Support**
**24/7 Helpline Resources:** Direct access to suicide prevention and crisis lines

**Emergency Guidance:** Step-by-step support during difficult moments

**Safety Planning Tools:** Create personalized safety plans

**Professional Care**
**Therapist Directory:** Search verified mental health professionals

**Direct Booking:** Schedule appointments seamlessly within the platform

**Specialist Matching:** Find therapists based on your specific needs

**Wellness Resources**
**Self-Help Articles:** Curated content on mental health topics

**Wellness Apps Integration:** Recommended tools for holistic care

**Educational Materials:** Learn about mental health conditions and treatments

**Prerequisites**
